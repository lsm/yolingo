Yolingo lets you relearn languages doing what you already do online.
1. Enter the URL of a Spanish website
2. Highlight words or phrases you don't understand to see its meaning
3. You will occasionally see those words with an icon later
4. Click the word to launch a minigame which lets you prove your knowledge
5. Rack up points from your words and rediscover your knowledge
